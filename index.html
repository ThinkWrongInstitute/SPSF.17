Origin


Architect Samuel Mockbee started running Rural Studio in 1993 which brought young architects to rural Alabama. They would design houses, communities, baseball elds while living with the locals, and understanding their actual needs.
Inspired by the Rural Studio, John Bielenberg started Project M in 2003. Project M is a program designed to take young creative people into unfamiliar places, immerse themselves into a community, create a project, and execute it. Secret Project


Launching its first class in fall 2015, Secret Project is an immersive program allowing all students from all disciplines at California College of the Arts to work on real world challenges in partnership with clients, organizations, and companies in the Bay Area.

Greensboro Crush


In the name of Secret Project Spring Break, we escaped to Greensboro, Alabama in late March, 2015. Over the course of one week, we realized how important it is to be submerged in unfamiliar places with both familiar and unfamiliar people alike. Now, we would like to partner with groups of people to unlock the ingenuity inside of us to achieve greater impact. After all, we are all born ingenious, right?
