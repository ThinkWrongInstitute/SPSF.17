Architect Samuel Mockbee started running Rural Studio in 1993 which brought young architects to rural Alabama. They would design houses, communities, baseball elds while living with the locals, and understanding their actual needs.
Inspired by the Rural Studio, John Bielenberg started Project M in 2003. Project M is a program designed to take young creative people into unfamiliar places, immerse themselves into a community, create a project, and execute it.
